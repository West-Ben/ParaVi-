About 2.5D Visualization

This visualization uses parallax scrolling to simulate depth without GPU-heavy 3D rendering. Data points on different layers move at different speeds, creating an illusion of depth.

Features:

  • Multiple depth layers
  • Smooth mouse interaction
  • Data-driven visualizations
  • Touch-device support
